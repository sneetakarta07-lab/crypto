What to check before installing a crypto app

  • Official app source: Download only from official stores and verify publisher.
  • Security features: 2FA, device management, withdrawal whitelist.
  • Transparent fees: spot fee + withdrawal cost + spread.
  • App stability: Avoid apps that crash during volatility.

Beginner-friendly features that matter

Beginners benefit from simple buy/sell screens, clear portfolio tracking, and easy history export. If you plan to learn more, an app that supports limit orders can reduce emotional mistakes.

Mobile security tips (Japan users)
